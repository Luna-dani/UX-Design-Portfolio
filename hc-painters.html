H&C Painters Home Improvements

Building a professional online presence for a Central New Jersey painting business

Overview

H&C Painters Home Improvements is a painting business based in Central New Jersey. The goal of this project was to design and develop a clean, professional website that showcases their services, builds trust with both residential and commercial clients, and drives more service inquiries.

Role

UX/UI Designer, Web Designer

  • UX/UI Design
  • Web Development
  • Content Strategy
  • Deployment & Testing

Project Details

Timeline: 2 weeks (2025)

Tools: Figma, Cursor, ChatGPT, GitHub, Vercel

Deliverables: Website design, responsive layout, improved information architecture, mobile-first design

Live Site: hcpainter.com
