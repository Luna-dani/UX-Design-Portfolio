About Me

Crafting Digital Experiences with Purpose

Hi, I'm Danielle Luna, a UX/UI designer passionate about crafting meaningful digital experiences that solve real user problems. With a background in both design and user research, I approach every project with empathy, curiosity, and attention to detail.

I specialize in mobile app design, responsive web design, and creating design systems that scale. My process combines user-centered research, iterative prototyping, and data-driven decision making to deliver solutions that truly resonate with users.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new design tools, reading about psychology and human behavior, or sketching ideas in my notebook. I believe that great design is invisible—it just works.
